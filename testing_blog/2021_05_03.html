Subway with Nicie

Met with Nicie to celebrate her birthday!

We went all the way to a Subway at Yishun because it was equally far from both of our houses. The day before, I did argue quite a bit with Ana over whether I should be getting the sugar-loaded double chocolate cookie.

  • Option 1: skip cookie + exercise + normal dinner
  • Option 2: eat cookie + exercise + normal dinner
  • Option 3: eat cookie + no-exercise + normal dinner
  • Option 4: eat cookie + no-exercise + skip dinner
  • Option 5: eat cookie + no-exericse + Simply Wrapps' salad for dinner

Option 1 was eliminated because I do want to eat a Subway double chocolate cookie. Haven't eaten it in sooooo looooong. Option 2 was out because I didn't want to keep thinking about burning excess calories while exercising. Ana never lets me off easily after red-light foods. Exercise is an enjoyment, not to be reduced to a calorie-balance. Option 3 was a no-no since the very beginning. I knew Ana would drive me crazy. Option 4 was discarded after some serious contemplation: Even though Ana would approve of this, I am afraid of losing my muscles if I skip dinner. Also, the state of starving is not a particularly pleasant one. Option 5 was the best one: I get to avoid Ana and keep my muscles (and stomach) happy.

The cookie was disappointing okay ... It would be better with less salt. Definitely wasn't as good as I rememebered it.

Dinner at Simply Wrapps

Dinner was decent. The cherry tomatoes were bursting with flavours. I love salads. They look so pretty. Most importantly, They make me feel safe.
